Who We Are

Genext's faculty is made up of scholars in the field of education, all of whom are committed to teaching. The faculty provide the essence of the learning experience at the institute. All of them have one vision in mind to impart quality education and be a leader in the field of education.

Why choose us

1. Career counselling

We ask students about their requirement and help them by assessing the pros and cons of the requirements.

2. Help choose the right course and right college

We also help students select the right university and the right course.

3. Entrance exam dates and notifications

One of the main roles played by us is to provide accurate information about the examination dates of various colleges and universities announced.

4. Free Counselling

We provide complete free counselling for the students and parents and information about various universities/courses.

5. Application Forms

We make available the required form for our students and help them in processing of application forms.

6. Admission Letter

Ensuring admission in the selected university, we obtain Admission Letter also for them.

7. Education Loan

Provide Guidance for applying to Banks for Education Loan.

8. Remittance of Fees

Lastly we assist the student to remit the fees to the concerned college/university.
